# 🚀 New StoryCanvas Update: maps, export options, and improvements for your writing workflow

We’re continuing to work so that StoryCanvas becomes an increasingly complete, flexible, and comfortable platform to use.  
In this update, we’ve added a **new map creation tool**, **more options for exporting your books**, **new templates for character sheets**, and several important improvements to tools many of you already use every day.

---

## 🗺️ New: Map Creator

StoryCanvas now includes a new tool to **create, edit, and save maps** directly within the platform.  
You’ll be able to work with different visual elements, organize your ideas more effectively, and build settings that support the development of your story.

In addition, this tool includes a **map library**, so you can save your projects and continue working on them whenever you want.

## 📚 New: More formats to export your books

We’ve expanded the export options so you have more freedom when sharing, backing up, or preparing your work for different platforms.

You can now export your book in new formats such as:

- `DOCX`
- `PDF`
- `EPUB`
- `TXT`

- `Kindle (HTML)`

We also improved the export settings to give you more control over the final output of your book.

We’ve improved the way the **StoryCanvas Assistant** works so it can better understand the context of your book and provide more useful responses.

With these improvements, the assistant can better interpret your project’s information and respond more accurately about characters, places, chapters, and other important elements of your story.

This translates into a smoother experience and responses that are more aligned with the actual content of your work.

- A change was added to prevent the page from automatically translating with Google Translate. This caused an error that erased texts in the chapters section and prevented writing. ⚠️

### Note:
If you still experience this issue, we suggest taking the following steps:

- 🔄 **Reload the page** to get the update.

- Fixed an error that prevented creating a task in the **Boards** tool with more than 200 characters.

# 🚀 New Update in StoryCanvas: Improved Writing Experience Like Notion! 🎉  

We have enhanced the **Notes, Covers, Characters, and Places** sections, introducing a more dynamic and intuitive editing experience. Now, each paragraph features a **side menu** and a **floating toolbar**, allowing you to transform and format your content more flexibly.  

## ✨ New Editing Options  

### 🔹 Side menu for each paragraph:  
- **Normal text**  
- **Headings** (different levels)  
- **Ordered and unordered lists**  
- ✅ **Checklist**  
- ``` Code ```  
- 📊 **Tables**  
- 🧮 **Mathematical formulas**  

### 🔹 Floating toolbar when selecting text:  
- 🔄 **Change text type** (heading, list, normal text, etc.)  
- 🔗 **Add links**  
- **Bold, italic, underline**  
- 🎨 **Change text color**  
- `Inline code`  
- 🔖 **Highlight text**  
- 🔠 **Change text case** (UPPERCASE, lowercase, Capitalized...)
